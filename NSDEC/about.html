About Us

National Skill Development Education Center India(NSDEC)

National Skill Development Education Center India(NSDEC) Delhi under the legislation of Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India Skills and knowledge are the driving forces of economic growth and social development for any country. Presently, the country faces a demand – supply mismatch, as the economy needs more ‘skilled’ workforce than that is available. In the higher education sphere, knowledge and skills are required for diverse forms of employment in the sector of education, health care manufacturing and other services. Potentially, the target group for skill development comprises all those in the lab our force, including those entering the lab our market for the first time, those employed in the organized sector and also those working in the unorganized sector.

  • Upgrade skills to international standards through significant industry involvement and develop necessary frameworks for standards, curriculum and quality assurance.
  • Enhance, support and coordinate private sector initiatives for skill development through appropriate Public-Private Partnership ( PPP ) models; strive for significant operational and financial involvement from private sector..
  • Play the role of a ‘market-maker’ by bringing funds, particularly in sectors where market mechanisms are ineffective or missing.

This scheme will support the unemployed person to free from various anti- social activities as well as to keep them in peace with the useful occupation. Under the vocational training programmer will be offered to the educated and the uneducated unemployed persons to start any help employment project or to seek any job opportunities in the government or in the private sectors E.g. Health Care, Agriculture, Technology, Fire Safety, Travel and Tourism, Shipping, Business and Commerce, Computer Science, IT, Library Science and many more…
